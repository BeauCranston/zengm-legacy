Association Recruiting Budgets

Your recruiting cash is given to you by the school. It is based on the number of players who have left school that year and you get $1000 per national tournament game that teams in your conference play in.

Prestige is from 0 to 100. The higher your prestige the easier it is to recruit players. It is impacted by regular season, conference tournament, and national tournament performance
