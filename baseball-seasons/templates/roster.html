Drag and drop row handles to move players and override the coach.

The first 8 hitters are the starting lineup (by batting order) and the rest are your bench. The first 5 pitchers are your starters (ace to fifth), next is your closer, then your bench. After the active roster you have your farm system (the farm system isn't simulated, but those players are developing).

The coach will pick your starting 8 positions after your first game, but you can override the coach by using the POS column.
