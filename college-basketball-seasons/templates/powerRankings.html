Power Rankings

The "AP Top 25" rating is the national ranking based on polling. The AP ranking is broken up into a "Performance" ranking based on point differential and recent team performance, and a "Talent" ranking based on player ratings and stats. The "RPI" rating is based on team winning percentage, opponent winning percentage, and opponent opponent winning percentage. The "SOS" ratings is a measure of schedule strength based on opponent winning percentage and opponent opponent winning percentage. The "Power" rating is based on point differential, opposing team point differential, and opposing opposing team point differential.
